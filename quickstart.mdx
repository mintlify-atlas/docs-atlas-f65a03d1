# Quickstart

This guide will walk you through the essential Orange Juice features and show you how to get the most out of the extension.

<Info>
  Make sure you've [installed Orange Juice](/installation) before following this guide.
</Info>

## Your first Orange Juice session

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Hacker News">
    Navigate to [https://news.ycombinator.com/](https://news.ycombinator.com/)
    
    You'll immediately notice subtle enhancements to the interface, including improved comment styling and navigation elements.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enable dark mode">
    Look for the dark mode toggle in the top navigation bar. Click it to switch between light and dark themes.
    
    The dark mode preserves Hacker News' visual structure while improving contrast and readability.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View keyboard shortcuts">
    Press `?` on any HN page to see all available keyboard shortcuts.
    
    This opens a modal with a comprehensive list of navigation shortcuts you can use to browse HN more efficiently.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Essential features

### Inline reply to comments

One of the most useful features is the ability to reply to comments without leaving the page.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open a comment thread">
    Click on any story's comment link to view the discussion.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click reply">
    Click the "reply" link under any comment. Instead of navigating to a new page, an inline reply form appears directly below the comment.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Quote selected text (optional)">
    Before clicking reply, you can select text from the comment. When you click reply, the selected text will be automatically quoted in your reply.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Write and submit">
    Type your reply in the textarea. The textarea automatically resizes as you type. Click "add comment" to submit.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  The inline reply feature saves time and keeps you in context while participating in discussions.
</Tip>

### Keyboard navigation

Navigate stories and comments efficiently without touching your mouse.

#### On story listing pages

<CodeGroup>
```text Story navigation
j / k       Navigate to next/previous story
o / Enter   Open story link
c           Open comments
v           Upvote story
u           Un-vote story
h           Hide story
?           Show keyboard shortcuts help
```

```text Comment navigation
j / k       Navigate to next/previous comment
p           Navigate to parent comment
o / Enter   Open comment link
r           Reply to comment
v           Upvote comment
u           Un-vote comment
?           Show keyboard shortcuts help
```
</CodeGroup>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Try navigating stories">
    On the front page, press `j` to move down to the next story, and `k` to move up to the previous story.
    
    The selected story will be highlighted with a visual indicator.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Open a story or comments">
    With a story selected, press `o` to open the story link in a new tab, or `c` to open the comments.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Navigate comments">
    In a comment thread, use `j` and `k` to navigate between comments. Press `r` to reply to the selected comment inline.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Hide read stories

Focus on new content by hiding stories you've already visited.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the checkbox">
    On any story listing page (front page, newest, best, etc.), look for the "Hide read stories" checkbox at the top of the page.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enable hiding">
    Check the box to hide all stories you've previously visited. This uses your browser's history to determine which links you've seen.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="See only new content">
    The page will update to show only stories you haven't opened yet, making it easier to catch up on new submissions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Your preference is remembered, so the checkbox will stay checked across sessions.
</Note>

### Unread comment highlighting

Never miss new replies in discussions you're following.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit a comment thread">
    Open any story with comments. Read through the discussion.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Return later">
    Come back to the same thread within 3 days.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="See new comments highlighted">
    New comments added since your last visit will be highlighted with an orange left border. Hover over them to see an even brighter highlight.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Orange Juice tracks which comments you've read locally in your browser. Comment read state is remembered for 3 days.
</Info>

### User information on hover

Quickly view user profiles without navigating away from the page.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Hover over a username">
    On any HN page, hover your mouse over any username (in comments, stories, or elsewhere).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View user details">
    A popup appears showing:
    - Account creation date
    - Karma score
    - Number of submissions
    - User's about section
    
    New users (less than 1 month old) are highlighted in green.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click links in about">
    Move your mouse over the popup to keep it open. Any links in the user's about section are clickable.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Favorite items

Save comments and stories for later reference.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the favorite button">
    Next to the "favorite" link on comments and stories, you'll see an enhanced favorite button.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click to favorite">
    Click the button to save the item. The action happens in place without redirecting you to a new page.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Access your favorites">
    View all your favorited items by visiting your profile and clicking the "favorites" link.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Advanced features

### Mermaid diagram rendering

Orange Juice automatically renders Mermaid diagrams in comments.

<Accordion title="How to write Mermaid diagrams in HN comments">
  Wrap your Mermaid diagram in `<mermaid>` tags with two leading spaces on every line inside the block:
  
  ```html
  <mermaid>
    graph TD
      A[Start] --> B[Process]
      B --> C[End]
  </mermaid>
  ```
  
  The diagram will be automatically rendered for users with Orange Juice installed.
</Accordion>

### Past page date picker

Explore Hacker News history with an interactive date picker.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to /front">
    Visit [https://news.ycombinator.com/front](https://news.ycombinator.com/front)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Use the date picker">
    You'll see an interactive date picker that lets you select any year since 2007, month, and day.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View historical front pages">
    Select a date to see what was on the Hacker News front page on that day. The picker defaults to yesterday and prevents selecting future dates.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Enhanced submission experience

Submitting stories is easier with character counting and automatic title fetching.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to submit page">
    Click "submit" in the HN navigation to create a new story submission.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Use the fetch title button">
    After entering a URL, click the "fetch title" button that appears next to the URL field. Orange Juice will automatically retrieve and populate the title from the URL.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Watch the character counter">
    As you type your title, a character counter appears showing how many characters remain until you reach the 80-character limit.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Profile quick access

Access your profile pages without leaving the current page.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click your username">
    In the top navigation bar, click your username to reveal a dropdown menu.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Navigate to profile sections">
    The dropdown provides quick access to:
    - Your profile
    - Submissions
    - Comments
    - Hidden items
    - Upvoted items
    - Favorites
  </Step>
</Steps>

### More hidden links

Access HN URLs that aren't exposed in the regular interface.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the 'more links' dropdown">
    Look for the "more links" dropdown in the top navigation bar.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Explore hidden pages">
    Click it to see a list of useful HN pages that aren't normally linked in the navigation, such as:
    - Leaders (karma leaderboard)
    - Best comments
    - Active discussions
    - Classic posts
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tips for power users

<Accordion title="Collapse comment threads efficiently">
  - Click on the indent area (left margin) of any comment to collapse/expand that thread
  - On thread pages, use the "collapse root" button to collapse all top-level comments at once
  - Navigate collapsed threads with keyboard shortcuts: `j` and `k` skip collapsed comments
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Combine features for maximum efficiency">
  - Use `j`/`k` to navigate stories, then `c` to open comments
  - Enable "Hide read stories" and refresh the page throughout the day to see only new content
  - Hover over usernames in discussions to quickly assess credibility and expertise
  - Use inline reply with text selection to quote specific points in lengthy comments
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Track ongoing discussions">
  - Favorite stories with active discussions you want to follow
  - Use unread comment highlighting to catch up on new replies when you return
  - Visit favorited items from your profile dropdown for quick access
</Accordion>

## What's next?

Now that you're familiar with Orange Juice's core features, explore the extension at your own pace. Most features activate automatically as you browse HN, requiring no configuration.

</Tip>

Happy browsing!
