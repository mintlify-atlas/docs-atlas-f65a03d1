## Design Philosophy

The goal isn't to redesign Hacker News. Its minimal UX works, and no one wants to relearn an interface that already does the job. Instead, Orange Juice focuses on:

- **Subtle enhancements** that feel natural and expected
- **Preserving the original design** while adding polish
- **Improving engagement** without overwhelming changes
- **Accessibility and performance** in all features

Ideally, you shouldn't really notice it at all. The features should feel subtle but natural: visible, useful, and aligned with what most users would expect the site to have supported in the first place.
